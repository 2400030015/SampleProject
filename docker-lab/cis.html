Overview

Computer Information Systems (CIS) combines business and technology to manage and improve computer-based systems. It focuses on solving real-world business problems using IT solutions.

Career Paths

  • Systems Analyst
  • Database Administrator
  • IT Consultant
  • Network Administrator
  • Cybersecurity Analyst
